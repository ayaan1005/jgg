# Creating A Server

## Head Over To The Dashboard [**HERE**](https://control.guitarhost.tech)

Login To The **Dashboard**  


## After Login You Will Be Redirected To The Main Dashboard Page

`1.` Now Scroll Down And Click On **View your servers** Button.  
 `2.` You Will Be Redirected To Your Server List Page.  
 `3.` Now Click On **Create a new server** Button, Then You Will Be Redirected To A Page Like This  
    
`4.` Select Your Specification Of Your Pterodactyl Server.  
**Free Plane**  
`RAM : 1024MB, DISK : 2048MB, CPU : 50%`  
 `5.` After Choosing The Specifications Click On **Create** Button.
