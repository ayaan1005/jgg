# Creating Account On The Panel

### Head Over To The Dashboard **[HERE](https://guitarhost.tech)**

Now The Interface Will Be Like This
![Dashboard](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/847221038872788992/847541911719903272/screenshot.png)

`1.` Click On **DISCORD** Button It Will Redirect You To Discord Authorization Page, Click On Authorize.<br>
`2.` After You Are Authorised You Will Be Redirected To Your Dashboard Main Page.<br>
`3.` Setup Password, An Email Will Be Sent To Your Gmail Account Linked To Your Discord Account. Like This<br>
![Email](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/847221038872788992/847543271298170910/Screenshot_2021-05-28-00-04-12-65.jpg)<br>
`4.` Click On **Setup Your Account** And You Will Be Redirected To The Password Page, Set Your Password And You Are Done.
